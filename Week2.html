    HODEY Vital Zentrum

    HODEY Viltal Zentrum has been working as a comprehensive provider in the healthcare sector for over 45 years in the Region of the low Rhine in cities like Kamp_lintfort, Kleve, Aachen and many more. In addition to tradition and experience, HODEY also stands for innovative customer solutions. They show as a very nice process in orthopedia how to use some tools of digital fabrication in the production of prosthetics, orthotics, and shoes.
