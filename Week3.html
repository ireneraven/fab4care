    BACHE INNOVATIVE

    Bache Innovative The Strickerei Bache was founded in 1927 by Otto Bache in Apolda / Thuringia, the then German knitting fortress. It is noteworthy for this company, that in 2006 the manufacturing process of "totally seamless products" was introduced.At the moment, the company includes 19 computer-controlled flat knitting machines. In my opinion, this company is of great interest, since when designing models with 3D machines, seamless ... the possibilities of making special designs with the objective of covering different needs increases enormously.
